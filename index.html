About Me

I'm Kate, a professional with a background in advocacy, project management, and digital fundraising, with a penchant for technology. My career journey has seen me significantly increase the digital income and list size for a several clients, lead the development of a voter education website, manage websites and social media for campaigns and nonprofits, and establish efficient CRM data systems across various fields. As a digital strategist, my initial exposure to HTML while designing emails marked the beginning of a profound interest in coding and web development. This passion led me to pursue further education, culminating in earning my Professional Certificate in Coding: Full Stack Development with MERN from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Drawing from my extensive experience in project management, community engagement, and digital strategy, combined with my web development training, I am well-equipped to create user-focused, versatile, and visually appealing websites and digital programs to cater to diverse client needs.

I live in Boston with my fiance, Ben, and our dog, Buster. When I'm not coding or designing, I love hiking, reading, trying new restaurants, and imagining how I would redesign houses on Zillow.
